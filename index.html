The Internet.

What is the Internet?

The Internet is a global network that allows for the sharing of information and communication between computers and devices. It was initially developed in the 1960s to enable government researchers to share resources and information, allowing for the easy exchange of data and collaboration across vast distances

Why was it made?

The Internet was created as a way for government researchers and academics to share information and resources more efficiently. It was intended to facilitate easier communication and data exchange, especially for national defense and scientific research. Over time, the Internet evolved into a global communication tool that is now used by billions of people around the world for various purposes such as communication, commerce, education, and entertainment.

When was it made?

It was made in the 1960s
